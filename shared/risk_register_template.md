# Shared — Risk Register Template

## Purpose
Track identified risks, their likelihood and impact, and the mitigation or acceptance decision for each.

| Risk ID | Description | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Severity | Mitigation | Owner | Status |

## Risk Categories
- **Technical**: Architecture, integration, performance, security risks.
- **Schedule**: Timeline and dependency risks.
- **Resource**: Personnel, budget, tooling risks.
- **External**: Market, regulatory, vendor, competitive risks.
- **Operational**: Process, communication, governance risks.

## Severity Calculation
Severity = Likelihood × Impact

| | Low Impact | Medium Impact | High Impact | Critical Impact |
|-|-----------|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| **High Likelihood** | Medium | High | Critical | Critical |
| **Medium Likelihood** | Low | Medium | High | Critical |
| **Low Likelihood** | Low | Low | Medium | High |

## Mitigation Strategies
- **Avoid**: Change the plan to eliminate the risk entirely.
- **Reduce**: Take actions that lower likelihood or impact.
- **Transfer**: Shift the risk to a third party (insurance, contract, vendor SLA).
- **Accept**: Acknowledge the risk and prepare a response if it materializes.

## Risk Review Schedule
Risks with High or Critical severity must be reviewed at every phase gate.
Medium risks reviewed at every other phase gate.
Low risks reviewed at plan completion.
