# Shared — Decision Quality Matrix

## Purpose
Score the quality of a decision or plan on multiple dimensions to produce a comparable, auditable quality rating.

## Quality Dimensions (each scored 1–10)

| # | Dimension | What it measures |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Goal Clarity | Is the goal stated with measurable success criteria? |
| 2 | Evidence Quality | Do key claims meet evidence standards? |
| 3 | Option Coverage | Were alternatives genuinely considered? |
| 4 | Assumption Transparency | Are assumptions explicit and challenged? |
| 5 | Risk Completeness | Are material risks identified and mitigated? |
| 6 | Dependency Mapping | Are cross-system/team dependencies documented? |
| 7 | Reversibility Awareness | Is the reversibility of the decision assessed? |
| 8 | Bias Clearance | Has SOPHIA cleared the plan of blocking biases? |
| 9 | Ethics Clearance | Has SOPHIA issued ethics clearance? |
| 10 | Implementation Feasibility | Can the plan actually be executed with available resources? |

## Scoring Rules
- Scores are assigned by SOPHIA, not LEON.
- A score of 1–3 on any dimension is a WARNING.
- A score of 1–2 on dimensions 8 or 9 (bias/ethics) is BLOCKING.
- Overall quality score = average of all 10 dimensions.
- Minimum acceptable overall score for synthesis: **7.0**.
