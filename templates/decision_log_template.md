```

- **Goal**: —
- **Recommendation**: —
- **Rationale**: —
- **Key Assumptions**: —
- **Open Advisories**: [list or "none"]
- **Arbitration IDs**: [list or "none"]
- **Escalation ID**: [ESC-number or "none"]
- **Status**: Active / Superseded by DEC-[number] / Implemented / Cancelled
```
